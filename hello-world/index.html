Well, I should at least get an introduction post in here. Right?

My name is Ron, and I like to do a terrible job at web development, and making Frankenstein code projects. I did live, and house my terrible monsters over at GitHub. But, they changed their password policy while I was gone, and now I'm locked out of my account. Which is whatever. My only issue there is I have spent a week talking to a support person, and they know its me, and I own the account, but they still won't send me the reset link.

Anyways, I'm just another person. I'm not famous in my field, or super gifted, I don't even have any popular projects I've started, or helped with. What I do have is a computer, accounts on GitHub and GitLab, an unhealthy need to have multiple websites, and an unsupported feeling of importance. I mean I even went through the trouble to set up a Ghost blog as a static site for GitLab/GitHub Pages.

Clearly, I am committed to making myself look like a fool, or super important, by writing bad articles, and updates on my projects, or whatever else tickles my fancy.

So, yeah, I'm sorry... buckle up, put on your helmet, and get ready for this mess!
